About Us

"Driven by Expertise,
Committed to Quality and Growth"

Welcome to Cropsy Nutrients Founded in 2017 in Gadwal District, Telangana, Cropsy Nutrients is a forward-thinking agricultural company dedicated to providing farmers with the essential nutrients needed to grow healthy, high-quality crops. Our company was established by a team of industry experts with over 25 years of experience in the agriculture input sector. We combine our deep expertise with a passion for enhancing farming productivity and sustainability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life of the growing population by providing Indian farmers with better fertilizers, including Micronutrients, Biofertilizers, and Plant Growth Regulators. We are committed to helping farmers grow healthy and affordable food, ensuring sustainability and improved yields for the future.

Our Certifications

We pride ourselves on adhering to the highest industry standards and continuously ensuring that our products and practices meet rigorous quality requirements. Our certifications demonstrate our commitment to quality, sustainability, and excellence in everything we do.



100% Organic Certified

Our products are certified to be 100% organic, ensuring that we only provide nature-friendly and chemical-free solutions.



ISO 9001:2015 Certified

We are committed to providing high-quality products and services, with a proven and internationally recognized system for managing quality.



UGAFS Accredited

As an accredited organization, we align with the best agricultural standards for safety, sustainability, and product performance.



MNS Certified

We uphold industry regulations and produce products that are certified to deliver reliable and effective solutions.
